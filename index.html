About Me

Hi, I'm Nick. I have a degree in Business Administration - Marketing from Augustana College where I played 4 years of varsity football. After graduation, I went to China for 4 months to teach English to high school students and play semi-pro football for 2 different teams.

After spending 4 months in China, I decided it was time to come back and get into my marketing career. I worked a couple customer service and sales focused part time jobs while looking for a full time position. In June of 2016 I was fortunate enough to find such a position as a Marketing Coordinator at Guaranteed Rate where I have been ever since. While at GR, I have taken on many different roles as a trainer, database expert, and just overall jack of all trades. Throughout my time at GR, I've had time to realize that I wanted to transition into web design.
